Course Info

Submitted to engineers from the Nile Pharmaceutical Company and Factory in cooperation with the Global Academy for Science and Knowledge

At the end of the training, you will obtain the following documented certificates
1- A certificate accredited by the Nile Company for training, and the number of approved hours is 50 training hours, accredited by the Nile Pharmaceutical Company, which is a government company affiliated with the business sector. 2- A statement addressed to whomever it may concern... stating that the number of approved training hours is 50 training hours - to be submitted at the college or at work to be deducted from the training hours. 3- An accredited certificate from the Global Academy of Science and Knowledge

First: Once the training on the platform is completed, an online copy (soft copy) of the certificates will be sent via email.

Second: The (original) paper copy of the certificates can be received from the administrative headquarters of Global Academy in Cairo, or you can request that it be sent to you by shipping it to your home, and it will be delivered to you within a week of your request for home shipping.

Training content
- Introduction to the divisions of electrical systems within factories - Design and operation of electrical panels in factories - Medium voltage panels and distributors - Low voltage panels - Operating electrical breakers and fuses - Maintenance of electrical breakers and fuses - Guards and controllers for electrical power bars and switch gears - Electrical workshops inside factories (motor winding and sizing) - Distribution and operation of emergency generators in factories - Diesel Generator - Maintenance of emergency generators in factories.
